The story of this website

This website started as a fun project for a university course in 2024, but then it took on a life of its own. Thanks to numerous suggestions and contributions over the years, it kept growing and improving, and managed to win a small niche of fans. Thanks for all the continuous support! We hope this little corner of Web can bring some peace and serenity in the turbulent seas of the Internet.

Please do get in touch if you have any questions, using the links on this page. Bugs and feature requests can be raised directly in Github's Issues section.
